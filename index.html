⚡ Key Things To Know About ROM PvP / GvG Damage ▼
  • Damage is calculated multiplicatively through layers. The most common stats are Pen / Magic Pen, Elemental, Racial, and Skill DMG.
  • Every layer has a cap of 85% damage reduction and +120% damage dealt.
  • Pen / Magic Pen is calculated first. Everything after it multiplies against whatever survives, making them high priority.
  • Elemental Reduction is one of the best investments. Just matching your opponent's elemental damage / reductions brings you to 50% — this is the highest stat for a neutral matchup, and it hits the 85% damage reduction cap faster than any other layer.
  • These stats follow a non-linear curve. Returns diminish the further you push toward either extreme. The closer you are to the breakeven point, the more value each percent gives.

Assumes +15 weapon and both accessories
